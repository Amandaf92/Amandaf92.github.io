Amanda Fockler

Hi there! I'm Amanda, a photographer and student at Oakland Community College. My passion lies in creatively capturing moments of love and self-expression. I aim to empower and celebrate every person I work with. As a member of the LGBTQ+ community I understand the importance of representation and visibility. I believe that everyone deserves to have their story told and I'd love to help tell yours.
